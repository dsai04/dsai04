## 📌 Featured Projects

### 📊 [Employee Performance & Workforce Analysis](https://github.com/dsai04/Employee_performance_analysis)
**Python · Pandas · Seaborn · Power BI**
- Identified 1,223 employees (40.8% of active workforce) as a flight-risk cohort: 5+ year tenure, strong ratings, zero promotions
- Found attrition spread across *all* performance bands (PIP: 27%, Exceeds: 17%) — a systemic retention issue, not a performance management one
- Built a 4-page Power BI dashboard on top of a Python pipeline that ranked 3,000 records by salary using window functions

### 🛍️ [Amazon Sales Data Analysis](https://github.com/dsai04/Amazon_Sales)
**Python · Pandas · SQL · Matplotlib · Jupyter Notebook**
- Found zero overlap between the Top 10 most-discounted and Top 10 highest-rated categories across 1,400+ items — deep discounts drive volume, not satisfaction
- Discovered budget-tier items consistently outrated premium-tier items, pointing to an expectation-calibration effect
- Built a weighted rating (rating × review volume) to rank categories more accurately than raw star ratings

### 📈 [Data Professional Survey Dashboard](https://github.com/dsai04/Data-professional-survey-dashboard)
**Power BI · Power Query**
- Analyzed 630 survey responses across 7 roles and 5 countries
- Found pay satisfaction (4.27/10) trails work-life balance (5.74/10) by 25%
- Segmented 42.7% of respondents as neutral on career-entry difficulty — likely candidates leveraging transferable domain skills

### 🏠 [Airbnb Pricing Analysis Dashboard](https://github.com/dsai04/Airbnb_pricing_dashboard)
**Tableau · Moving Averages**
- Found a U-curve in per-bedroom pricing across 2,580+ Seattle listings — mid-size properties earn 18% less per bedroom than either extreme
- Showed a 6-bedroom listing matches the per-bedroom efficiency of a 1-bedroom, suggesting 3-4BR hosts are mispricing
- Found premium ZIP codes earn ~45% above city average but carry 40-45% seasonal volatility, while below-average ZIPs stay flat

### 🗄️ [WorkDock — Relational Database Design](https://github.com/dsai04/WorkDock)
**Python · SQL · SQLite**
- Designed a normalized 3-table schema with parameterized queries to enforce referential integrity
- Separated recruiter and job-seeker data flows to simplify downstream querying and reporting
